About Foodalorie

Our Story

Foodalorie was founded in 2024 by a passionate team of nutritionists, tech developers, and food lovers. We noticed that while modern food delivery apps made ordering convenient, they made calorie counting extremely difficult. Restaurant food is often packed with hidden oils, sugars, and extra calories. We set out to build a platform where every single dish is standardized, verified by registered dietitians, and prepared by top chefs using wholesome ingredients.

Our Core Mission

We believe that a healthy lifestyle shouldn't feel like a chore. Our mission is to democratize nutritional literacy. We make calorie-conscious eating accessible, transparent, and delicious by providing detailed macro breakdowns (proteins, carbs, fats) for every meal, so you know exactly what is entering your body.

How It Works

  1. Choose Your Daily Plan: Select from Keto, Balanced, High-Protein, or Low-Carb diets, or set a specific calorie target (e.g., under 500 kcal per meal).
  2. Browse Verified Menus: Explore dishes from our restaurant partners. Every item shows its calorie count and macronutrient breakdown.
  3. Fresh Preparation: Our partner restaurants cook your order using fresh ingredients and exact portion controls.
  4. Swift & Care-filled Delivery: Our delivery team ensures your hot meal reaches you quickly and safely.
